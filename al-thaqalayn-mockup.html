Foundation Layer

Simple explanations, historical context, contemporary relevance

The opening phrase of the Quran, Bismillah ar-Rahman ar-Rahim, serves as both a divine key and a spiritual compass for Muslims.

"In the name of Allah, the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful"

This verse anchors every act, speech, or intention in Allah's sovereignty and grace. While Surah Al-Fatiha was revealed in Mecca during the early years of prophethood, Bismillah itself transcends time and place‚Äîit is repeated 114 times in the Quran and daily in prayers.
